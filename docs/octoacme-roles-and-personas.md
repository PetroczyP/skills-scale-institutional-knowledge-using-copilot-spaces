# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## QA Lead

### Role Summary
QA Leads oversee testing strategies and ensure quality standards are met throughout the development lifecycle. They coordinate testing activities and define quality criteria that guide development and release decisions.

### Responsibilities
- Oversee testing strategies and ensure quality standards are met
- Coordinate manual and automated testing activities with developers and PMs
- Define test coverage requirements and acceptance criteria validation
- Manage test environments and data
- Identify quality risks and propose mitigation strategies

### Goals
- Ensure high-quality releases with minimal defects
- Maximize test coverage and effectiveness
- Reduce time-to-detect and time-to-fix for issues

### Typical Communication
- Test strategy reviews with Developers and Product Managers
- Quality status updates in sprint reviews and release planning
- Test coverage reports and defect metrics
- Collaboration with Project Managers on timelines and release testing
- Acceptance criteria discussions with Product Managers

---

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers design and maintain build, deployment, and monitoring pipelines. They ensure efficient CI/CD processes and collaborate on incident response and system reliability.

### Responsibilities
- Design and maintain build, deployment, and monitoring pipelines
- Ensure efficient CI/CD processes
- Collaborate on incident response and system reliability
- Manage infrastructure as code and deployment automation
- Monitor system performance and optimize deployment workflows

### Goals
- Minimize deployment time and risk
- Maximize system uptime and reliability
- Enable fast, safe delivery of changes to production

### Typical Communication
- Build and deployment automation discussions with Developers
- Release readiness reviews with Project Managers
- Infrastructure planning and incident retrospectives
- Test environment coordination with QA Lead
- Monitoring and alerting setup with development teams

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers lead user experience design and ensure usability standards across products. They collaborate on prototypes and user testing to create intuitive, user-centered solutions.

### Responsibilities
- Lead user experience design and ensure usability standards
- Collaborate on prototypes and user testing efforts
- Create wireframes, mockups, and design specifications
- Validate designs with users and stakeholders
- Maintain design systems and accessibility guidelines

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, accessible user experiences
- Reduce user friction and increase satisfaction
- Ensure design consistency across products

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and feedback sessions with Developers
- Requirements gathering and validation with Product Managers
- Design timeline coordination with Project Managers
- Usability testing sessions and findings reports
- Design handoff documentation and specifications

---

## Customer Support

### Role Summary
Customer Support acts as the voice of users by surfacing feedback and pain points. They collaborate on post-release processes and provide critical insights that drive product improvements.

### Responsibilities
- Act as the voice of users by surfacing feedback and pain points
- Collaborate on post-release processes and user documentation
- Triage and escalate user-reported issues
- Provide insights for product improvements
- Track and analyze common support patterns and trends

### Goals
- Maximize user satisfaction and retention
- Reduce support ticket volume through product improvements
- Ensure timely resolution of user issues

### Typical Communication
- Feature feedback and user insights shared with Product Managers
- Bug reports and issue triage with Developers
- User documentation reviews and updates
- Support metrics and trend analysis reports
- Post-release validation and known issue tracking

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Business Analysts bridge technical and business goals, ensuring solutions align to measurable outcomes. They refine requirements and analyze data to inform strategic decisions.

### Responsibilities
- Bridge technical and business goals
- Refine requirements and ensure solutions align to measurable outcomes
- Analyze data and metrics to inform decisions
- Document business processes and workflows
- Validate that implementations meet business objectives

### Goals
- Ensure alignment between technical solutions and business value
- Maximize return on investment for development efforts
- Enable data-driven decision making

### Typical Communication
- Requirements refinement sessions with Product Managers
- Technical feasibility discussions with Developers
- Scope and planning reviews with Project Managers
- Business case documentation and ROI analysis
- Metrics dashboards and outcome tracking reports
